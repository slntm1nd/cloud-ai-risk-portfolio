## Business Context
Assessment of an AI-powered system processing user data in a regulated environment.

## Scope
- Data privacy and protection
- Model transparency and explainability
- Bias and fairness
- Security and access controls
- Third-party/vendor dependency

## Key Risks Identified
- Potential exposure of sensitive data
- Lack of human oversight in decision-making
- Model drift and performance degradation
- Vendor lock-in and limited transparency

## Risk Evaluation Approach
- Likelihood vs impact analysis
- Mapping risks to governance and compliance controls
- Alignment with emerging Responsible AI principles

## Recommended Controls
- Human-in-the-loop validation
- Data minimization and access restrictions
- Continuous monitoring and logging
- Periodic model review and revalidation
- Vendor due diligence and contract controls

## Outcome
Created a documented, audit-ready AI risk assessment with clear remediation recommendations.
