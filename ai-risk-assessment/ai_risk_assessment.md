# AI Risk Assessment & Governance - Chatbot System (Sample Project)

**Objective:** Evaluate AI-related risks and governance for a customer support chatbot.

## 1. AI System Description
- **System:** AI-powered customer support chatbot
- **Purpose:** Answer customer questions using company documentation

## 2. Data Sources
| Data Source        | Risk               |
|--------------------|--------------------|
| Internal documents | Data leakage       |
| User input         | Prompt injection   |
| Training data      | Bias               |

## 3. AI Risk Identification
| Risk Category   | Description                  |
|----------------|-------------------------------|
| Bias           | Unequal responses             |
| Hallucinations | Incorrect information         |
| Privacy        | Exposure of sensitive data    |
| Security       | Model misuse                  |

## 4. AI Act Classification (EU)
AI system classified as **Limited Risk** under EU AI Act.  
Transparency obligations apply.

## 5. Risk Assessment
| Risk            | Likelihood | Impact | Risk Level|
|-----------------|------------|--------|-----------|
| Hallucinations  | High       | Medium | High      |
| Data leakage    | Medium     | High   | High      |
| Bias            | Medium     | Medium | Medium    |

## 6. Mitigation Controls
| Risk            | Control                     |
|-----------------|-----------------------------|
| Hallucinations  | Human oversight             |
| Data leakage    | Data filtering              |
| Bias            | Regular audits              |

## 7. Residual Risk
Residual AI risks remain Medium and require ongoing monitoring.

## 8. AI Governance Recommendations
- Establish AI usage policy  
- Define accountability and human oversight  
- Implement monitoring and review cycles
